# Scoutpost pricing

Hosted Scoutpost uses monthly credits.

## Plans

- **Free**: $0/month, 100 credits
- **Pro**: $10/month, 1,000 credits
- **Team**: $50/month, 5,000 shared credits, plus 1,000 credits per seat

## Plan features

- **Free**: Page, Beat, and Social Scouts; scheduled email alerts; scraped source data and source-linked findings stored in your workspace
- **Pro**: Everything in Free, plus Civic Scout, Fleet Scout, and tamper-evident Page Archive snapshots
- **Team**: Everything in Pro, with a shared newsroom credit pool and MuckRock-managed membership. Scouts remain individually owned.

## Typical run costs

- **Page Scout**: 1 credit
- **Beat Scout**: 7 credits
- **Social Scout**: 2 credits for Instagram, X, and TikTok; 7 for LinkedIn; 15 for Facebook
- **Civic Scout**: 10 credits; refunded when a run queues no documents
- **Fleet Scout**: 1 credit, plus 1 when natural-language criteria run

Always disclose spend before triggering expensive runs, especially Civic Scouts or batches of many scouts.
